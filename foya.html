Upcoming Events

FUNAAB Outstanding Youth Awards, FOYA is an award powered by Jasline Entertainment in recognition, acknowledgment, and celebration of bold and beautiful brains on campus.
The award idolizes greatness in different phases of undergraduates' life on the FUNAAB campus. It has categories ranging from awarding academic gods, entrepreneurship and business-oriented young minds, political forerunners, and other creatively worthy personalities on campus.
This significant event is to come to life annually during second semesters, be it midway or almost to end, to stimulate or wrap up each academic session for students.
Nominations are to be done individually and then collated for general purposes. This is the manifestation of reckoning with greatness amidst young minds. The great students are worthy of this, at least. As the award is tagged: FOYA, it is all Fo' Ya (for you).
